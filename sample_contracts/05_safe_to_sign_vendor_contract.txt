VENDOR SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Vendor Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of May 1, 2025 between GreenLeaf Organics LLC ("Client") and SupplyChain Pro Inc. ("Vendor"). Both parties have negotiated this Agreement in good faith and intend it to reflect a fair and balanced commercial relationship.

1. SERVICES
Vendor shall provide supply chain consulting and logistics management services as described in mutually agreed Statements of Work ("SOW"). Each SOW must be signed by authorized representatives of both parties before work commences. Neither party may unilaterally modify the scope of a signed SOW; any changes require a written amendment signed by both parties.

2. PAYMENT TERMS
Client shall pay Vendor within 30 days of receipt of a valid invoice. Invoices not disputed in good faith within 10 business days of receipt shall be deemed accepted. Late payments shall accrue interest at 1.5% per month on undisputed amounts. If Client disputes any portion of an invoice, it shall pay the undisputed portion on time and notify Vendor in writing of the specific basis for the dispute.

3. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
Each party retains ownership of its pre-existing intellectual property. Any work product created specifically and exclusively for Client under a SOW and fully paid for shall become Client's property upon full payment. Vendor retains the right to use general methodologies, tools, and know-how developed during the engagement for other clients, provided Vendor does not disclose Client's confidential information.

4. CONFIDENTIALITY
Each party shall treat the other party's confidential information with reasonable care, no less than it uses to protect its own confidential information. Neither party shall disclose the other's confidential information to third parties without prior written consent, except as required by law. This obligation continues for three (3) years following termination of the Agreement. Both parties acknowledge that information in the public domain is not confidential.

5. LIABILITY
Each party's total aggregate liability to the other under this Agreement, whether in contract, tort, or otherwise, shall not exceed the total fees paid or payable in the twelve (12) months preceding the claim. Neither party shall be liable for indirect, consequential, or punitive damages, except in cases of fraud or willful misconduct. This mutual limitation applies equally to both parties.

6. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this Agreement for convenience upon 30 days written notice to the other party. Either party may terminate immediately upon written notice if the other party materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure such breach within 15 business days of receiving notice. Upon termination, Client shall pay for all services performed and expenses incurred through the termination date. Vendor shall deliver all completed work product to Client within 10 business days of termination.

7. WARRANTIES
Vendor warrants that: (a) the services will be performed in a professional and workmanlike manner consistent with industry standards; (b) Vendor has the right to enter into and perform this Agreement; and (c) the services will not infringe any third-party intellectual property rights. Client warrants that it has the right to provide any materials or access required for Vendor to perform the services.

8. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
In the event of any dispute, the parties agree to first attempt resolution through good-faith negotiation for 30 days. If unresolved, the parties shall submit the dispute to non-binding mediation before pursuing arbitration or litigation. If mediation fails, disputes shall be resolved by binding arbitration under the AAA Commercial Rules, with one arbitrator, in Chicago, Illinois. Each party shall bear its own costs in arbitration; arbitration fees shall be split equally.

9. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Illinois. The parties agree that Illinois law applies without regard to its conflict of laws rules.

10. GENERAL
This Agreement may not be amended except by a written document signed by both parties. Neither party may assign this Agreement without the other party's prior written consent, which shall not be unreasonably withheld. This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the parties regarding its subject matter.

Client: GreenLeaf Organics LLC
Vendor: SupplyChain Pro Inc.
Date: May 1, 2025
