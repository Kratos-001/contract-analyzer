COMMERCIAL LEASE AGREEMENT

This Commercial Lease Agreement ("Lease") is entered into as of June 1, 2025 between Skyline Properties Group ("Landlord") and BrightMind Tutoring Center LLC ("Tenant").

PROPERTY: 2200 Commerce Boulevard, Suite 400, Austin, Texas 78701 ("Premises")
LEASE TERM: 36 months commencing June 1, 2025 and ending May 31, 2028
MONTHLY RENT: $6,800 per month

1. RENT AND PAYMENT
Tenant shall pay monthly rent on or before the 1st day of each calendar month. Rent not received by the 5th of the month shall incur a late fee of $250. Rent shall be paid by ACH transfer to Landlord's designated account. Rent shall increase by 3% on each anniversary of the Lease commencement date.

2. SECURITY DEPOSIT
Tenant shall deposit $13,600 (equivalent to two months' rent) as a security deposit upon execution of this Lease. Landlord shall hold the deposit in a segregated account. Upon termination, Landlord shall return the deposit within 30 days minus any deductions for unpaid rent or documented damage beyond normal wear and tear. Landlord shall provide an itemized written statement of any deductions.

3. USE OF PREMISES
Tenant may use the Premises solely for the operation of a tutoring center and related educational services. Tenant shall not use the Premises for any unlawful purpose. Tenant shall comply with all applicable laws, ordinances, and regulations. Tenant shall not make structural alterations to the Premises without Landlord's prior written consent, which shall not be unreasonably withheld.

4. MAINTENANCE AND REPAIRS
Landlord shall maintain the structural elements of the building, including roof, exterior walls, foundation, and common areas. Landlord shall maintain HVAC systems in working order. Tenant shall maintain the interior of the Premises in clean and good condition, and shall be responsible for interior repairs arising from Tenant's use. Each party shall promptly notify the other of any conditions requiring repair.

5. UTILITIES
Tenant shall pay for electricity and internet services consumed within the Premises. Landlord shall provide and pay for water, sewer, common area maintenance, and property taxes included in a monthly CAM charge of $800, subject to annual reconciliation based on actual costs with 30 days notice of adjustment.

6. INSURANCE
Tenant shall maintain commercial general liability insurance with limits of at least $1,000,000 per occurrence and $2,000,000 in aggregate, naming Landlord as an additional insured. Tenant shall maintain property insurance covering its personal property and leasehold improvements. Landlord shall maintain property insurance for the building structure. Each party shall provide the other with certificates of insurance upon request.

7. SUBLETTING AND ASSIGNMENT
Tenant shall not sublet the Premises or assign this Lease without Landlord's prior written consent. Landlord shall not unreasonably withhold, condition, or delay consent to a sublease or assignment to a financially qualified party for a permitted use. Landlord shall respond to any written request within 15 business days.

8. DEFAULT AND REMEDIES
Tenant shall be in default if: (a) Tenant fails to pay rent within 10 days of its due date; or (b) Tenant breaches any material term of this Lease and fails to cure such breach within 30 days of written notice from Landlord (or within a reasonable additional period if the breach cannot reasonably be cured in 30 days and Tenant has commenced cure). Upon default, Landlord may terminate this Lease and pursue all remedies available at law or in equity. Tenant shall be liable for rent through the end of the Lease Term, reduced by any amounts Landlord recovers through reasonable mitigation efforts.

9. EARLY TERMINATION
Tenant may terminate this Lease after the 18th month upon 90 days written notice to Landlord, provided Tenant pays an early termination fee equal to 3 months' base rent. Landlord may terminate this Lease only upon Tenant's uncured default or if Landlord elects to demolish or substantially renovate the building upon 180 days written notice and full refund of the security deposit.

10. RENEWAL OPTION
Tenant shall have one option to renew this Lease for an additional term of 24 months under the same terms, except that monthly rent shall be reset to the then-current market rate for comparable space in the area, mutually agreed by the parties or determined by an independent appraiser. Tenant must exercise this option in writing no later than 120 days before the Lease expiration.

11. GOVERNING LAW
This Lease is governed by the laws of the State of Texas. Any dispute shall be resolved in the state or federal courts of Travis County, Texas.

Landlord: Skyline Properties Group
Tenant: BrightMind Tutoring Center LLC
Date: June 1, 2025
